MechWarrior 2 MIDI soundtrack
Ripped by Infamous Star (infamousstargamerips.tumblr.com)

These are the MIDI tracks from MechWarrior 2. Sort of. DosBox has a feature that allows it to record raw MIDI events, and I used that to rip them from the game. Then, I cut them where they loop, and put each channel on its own track. That's all I did, so these should sound exactly like they do in-game. I've also included splash.mid -- the installer music from Windows versions of the game.
